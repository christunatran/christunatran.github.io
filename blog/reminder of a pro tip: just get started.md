i found it daunting to be assigned a 3-hour Unity tutorial for my Hypercinema class. i wrote down on my to-do list to just get started with 30 minutes of it. this way, i lowered the stakes for myself. now that i have a sense of what working with this new tool is like, and what the tutorial entails, it removes a lot of the uncertainty that was causing my paralysis toward getting started. i can go to bed tonight glad that i got started, and look forward to continuing tomorrow.

i've heard about strategies like "the 2-minute rule" time and time again, but in practice, often fail to remember it. 

hopefully writing this post will help to engrain it in my mind.
