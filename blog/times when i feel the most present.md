present:
- working with woodworking tools. listening to the hum and feeling the viibrations of the machinery makes me feel like i'm *really* there. the repetitive, tactile motions toward the vision of an idea brought to life. not being attentive could lead to losing a finger...
- driving. same idea where spacing out is literally dangerous.
- immersing myself into a good movie.

not present:
- my macbook with its elaborate trackpad gestures and shortcuts and windows and tabs which are meant to make my life easier but somehow contribute to my overwhelm with the immediacy and convenience they accustom me to. monkey brain wants thing now now now!!! it's scary to think about how the interfaces we use daily can mold and shape us by instilling habits that make their way into our real lives.
  - *computer time* doesn't hit like it used to... all of these windows and icons are distracting me from the initial goal… of getting done with what i’ve set out to do
