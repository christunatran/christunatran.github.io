are a lot harder to manage than they let on. a decent-looking fringe can require daily washing, a hair dryer, a hair roller, hair oil, and a straightener. all of that work just for it to get split apart by the wind. i once got a perm only on my fringe and those 3 weeks it lasted for were the best my bangs had ever been. without a perm, bang management is truly magic… 

anyone with a nice fringe has my respect. i see the effort involved.
