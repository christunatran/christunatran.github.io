ABOUT

I'm Christina. I often go by tunapee 🐟. I'm based in NYC. I'm a graduate student at NYU ITP (Interactive Telecommunications Program, AKA "art school for engineers"). I'm a Vietnamese-American human bean 🫘. I live with two cats named Chilly and Langley 🐈🐈. My interests are summarized with cats, coding, woodworking, locking dance, yoga, and coffee. I'm developing my creative practice and keeping an open mind to where it will take me-- maybe teaching, maybe new media art? Sometimes I am a barista and hold a somewhat frivolous Sensory Skills certification from the SCA. Before that, I was a part of the F2'24 batch at The Recurse Center where I made amazing friends. Before that, I was taking night classes in Industrial Design at LaGuardia Community College. Before that, I was working as a data scientist and consultant. Before that, I studied physics and computational modeling at Virginia Tech. Before that, I was growing up in Annandale, Virginia, playing the violin. I have a condition called Myasthenia Gravis, and am in remission as of December 2025. After beating this disease, I now feel like I can do anything! My life's motto is: "Start where you are, use what you have, do what you can."
